The long version

When the migration started we assumed the hardest part would be the data backfill, but the backfill finished in a weekend; the real cost was the six weeks of dual-write verification, where every mismatch had to be triaged as either a legitimate legacy bug, a race in the comparison harness, or an actual defect in the new path. Forty-one mismatches later, exactly three were real defects — and all three were in code that predated the migration.
