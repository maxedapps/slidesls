# Boardroom

Premium, calm authority: deep charcoal-navy surfaces, a brass/gold accent, Libre Franklin display type over Source Serif 4 body, hairline gold rules, and understated motion — slow fades and patient staggers.

## Voice

- Numbers earn the accent: brass highlights key figures, never decoration.
- Serif body copy reads as considered prose; keep sentences complete.
- Motion is patient — a deck that never hurries reads as a room that is already convinced.

## When not to use

- Playful launches or community content (use `pop`).
- Hands-on engineering material (use `terminal`).
