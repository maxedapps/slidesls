# Pop

Playful, loud, friendly: cream ground, coral + electric-blue accents, Bricolage Grotesque display type, thick ink borders with offset "sticker" shadows, and springy motion with real overshoot.

## Voice

- Poster energy: short punchy claims, big shapes, one joke per slide max.
- Two accents (coral, blue) — alternate them; never introduce a third.
- The bounce is the brand: keep the springy easing, keep borders thick.

## When not to use

- Executive or investor material (use `boardroom`).
- Long-form teaching narratives (use `editorial`).
